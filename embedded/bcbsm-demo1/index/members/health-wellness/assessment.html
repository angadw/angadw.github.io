Health Assessment

The Blue Cross® Health & Wellness health assessment, powered by WebMD®, is designed to help you get a picture of your current health and health risks. In an easy-to-read, interactive format, the health assessment asks you questions designed to evaluate your health.

After you complete your health assessment, you'll get the information you need to help you improve your health, including:

  • A health score based on your modifiable health risks
  • A list of your highest risk areas
  • A Modifiable Risk Report and a Condition Risk Report
  • A list of next steps you can take to improve your health

Taking the health assessment is easy. Here's how to get started:

  1. Log in to your account at bcbsm.com.
  2. Click Health & Wellness in the blue bar at the top to enter the Blue Cross Health & Wellness site. You'll need to register if this is your first time entering the site.
  3. Click Take Your Health Assessment on the home page to begin.
