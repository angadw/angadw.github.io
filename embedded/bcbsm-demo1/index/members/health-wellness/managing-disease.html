Chronic Condition Management


If you have a chronic condition like diabetes, asthma or heart disease, you might think that it's impossible to improve your quality of life.

But that's not true.

At Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan and Blue Care Network, we want to help you learn to live with your condition, so you can achieve the quality of life you want and deserve.

Managing your health

Blue Cross® Health & Wellness, powered by WebMD®, has a wealth of resources to help you live with health conditions, including tips, guides, quizzes and videos.

This information can help you talk with your doctor about your treatment options. Together, you can create a plan of action to manage your condition.

Log in to your account at bcbsm.com and click the Health & Wellness tab at the top to access the Blue Cross Health & Wellness site.

Our chronic condition management programs

Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan

When it comes to managing chronic conditions, Blue Cross Health & Wellness has Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan members covered. Both the complex and enhanced chronic condition management programs are designed to help you learn about illness and improve your quality of life.

These programs offer:

  • Telephone-based support from registered nurses
  • Condition-specific educational booklets
  • Chronic illness newsletters

If you're a Blue Cross Health & Wellness member, find out how to take the first steps toward chronic condition self-management by logging into your account at bcbsm.com.

Blue Care Network

Blue Care Network has partnered up with health care professionals to create chronic condition management programs.

These programs provide the education and support you need to manage your chronic condition. You'll develop management strategies that work for you and learn about your treatment options.

Want more information? Fill out our nurse callback form and we'll contact you.
